Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2025

Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect:

  • Your name and email address

  • Project details you provide in the form

If you make a payment, Stripe collects your payment information (card number, billing address) directly. We never see or store your full payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Process payments for services
  • Communicate about ongoing projects

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate this site:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions
  • Stripe — processes payments (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Cloudflare — provides web analytics and CDN services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

Data Transmission & Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections. Payment information is handled entirely by Stripe and is never stored on this site. We rely on PCI-compliant processors to handle sensitive financial data.
